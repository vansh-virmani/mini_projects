#  Fixora — Learn From Your Bugs

Fixora is an AI-powered platform that helps developers grow by turning their coding errors into learning opportunities.

Instead of just fixing bugs, Fixora helps you **understand why they happen** and **improve over time**.

---

##  How It Works

1. Paste your error  
2. AI analyzes the issue  
3. Identifies the missing concept  
4. Generates a daily challenge  
5. Tracks your weekly progress
